Raigen's Point Builder

Rules:

  • The builder is currently based off of a 32 point pool.
  • Stats can range from a min of 4 to a max of 20.
  • Bumping a stat from 8-13 decreases the pool buy 1.
  • 14-16 costs 2 points each increase.
  • 17 & 18 both cost 3 points, 19 & 20 both cost 4.
  • Any decreases will refund the associated points.
